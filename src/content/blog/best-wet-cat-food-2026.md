## Why Wet Food for Cats?

Cats benefit from wet food because:
- Higher moisture (cats don't drink enough water)
- Higher protein, lower carbs
- More palatable
- Helps with weight management

## FAQ

### How much wet food should I feed?

Typically 1 can (3oz) per 3-3.5 lbs of body weight daily.

### Wet vs dry for cats?

Wet is better for hydration and weight control. Many owners feed both.
