## The Great Grain Debate

Should you feed your dog grain-free or grain-inclusive food? Let's look at the science.

## What is Grain-Free?

Grain-free dog food excludes wheat, corn, rice, and other grains, often replacing them with potatoes or legumes.

## What is Grain-Inclusive?

Grain-inclusive food contains whole grains that provide carbohydrates, fiber, and nutrients.

## The DCM Connection

In 2018, the FDA investigated a potential link between grain-free diets and dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM) in dogs.

## Our Recommendation

Unless your dog has a diagnosed grain allergy (rare), grain-inclusive food is perfectly fine and often more affordable.

## FAQ

### Are grains bad for dogs?

No! Most dogs digest grains well. Grain allergies are uncommon.

### What grains are safe?

Rice, oats, barley, and quinoa are all safe and nutritious for dogs.
