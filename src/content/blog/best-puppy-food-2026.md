## Why Puppy Food Matters

Puppies have different nutritional needs than adult dogs. They need:

- **Higher protein** for muscle development
- **More calories** for energy and growth
- **DHA** for brain development
- **Calcium & phosphorus** for bone growth

## Puppy Feeding Guide

### How Much to Feed

| Age | Small Breed | Medium Breed | Large Breed |
|-----|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| 8-12 weeks | 1/2 - 1 cup | 1 - 2 cups | 2 - 3 cups |
| 3-6 months | 1 - 1.5 cups | 2 - 3 cups | 3 - 5 cups |
| 6-12 months | 1.5 - 2 cups | 3 - 4 cups | 5 - 7 cups |

*Always follow package guidelines and adjust based on your puppy's body condition.*

### Feeding Schedule

- **8-12 weeks:** 4 meals per day
- **3-6 months:** 3 meals per day
- **6-12 months:** 2 meals per day

## FAQ

### When should I switch from puppy to adult food?

Most puppies can transition to adult food at 12 months. Large breeds may need puppy food until 18-24 months.

### Is grain-free better for puppies?

Not necessarily. Grains provide important nutrients. Choose grain-free only if your puppy has a diagnosed allergy.

### Can I feed my puppy adult dog food?

No. Puppies need specific nutrients for proper growth. Adult food lacks the nutrition puppies need.
