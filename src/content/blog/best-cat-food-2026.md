## Buyer's Guide

### What to Look for in Cat Food

- **Protein source:** Cats are obligate carnivores - need high protein
- **AAFCO statement:** Ensures complete nutrition
- **Life stage:** Kitten, adult, or senior formulas
- **Wet vs Dry:** Wet food provides hydration, dry is convenient

## FAQ

### Is grain-free better for cats?

Cats don't need grains, but they're not harmful unless your cat has an allergy.

### How much should I feed my cat?

Follow the feeding guide on the package, adjusted for your cat's age and activity level.
