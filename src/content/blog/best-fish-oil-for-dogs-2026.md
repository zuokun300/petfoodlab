## What to Look For

### Quality Indicators

- ✅ **Wild-caught fish** (better than farmed)
- ✅ **Third-party tested** for purity
- ✅ **Molecularly distilled** (removes contaminants)
- ✅ **Clear EPA/DHA amounts** on label
- ✅ **Added vitamin E** (prevents oxidation)

### Red Flags

- ❌ **Vague sourcing** ("fish oil" without specifics)
- ❌ **No third-party testing**
- ❌ **Artificial colors or flavors**
- ❌ **Excessive fillers**

## FAQ

### How long until I see results?

Most dogs show improvement in 4-8 weeks. Skin and coat improvements may take 8-12 weeks.

### Can I give too much fish oil?

Yes. Excessive fish oil can cause diarrhea, weight gain, or affect blood clotting. Follow dosage guidelines.

### Liquid vs capsules - which is better?

Liquid is easier to dose and mix with food. Capsules are more convenient for travel. Choose based on your dog's preference.
