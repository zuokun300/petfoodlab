## Buyer's Guide

### What to Look for in Dog Food

- **Protein source:** Look for named meat (chicken, beef) not "meat by-products"
- **AAFCO statement:** Ensures complete nutrition
- **Life stage:** Puppy, adult, or senior formulas
- **Special needs:** Grain-free, limited ingredient, weight management

## FAQ

### Is grain-free better for dogs?

Not necessarily. Unless your dog has a grain allergy, grains can be a healthy source of carbohydrates.

### How much should I feed my dog?

Follow the feeding guide on the package, adjusted for your dog's activity level and body condition.
