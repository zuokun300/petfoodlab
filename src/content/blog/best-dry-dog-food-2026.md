## Buying Guide

### What to Look For

- **Named protein source** (chicken, beef, not "meat by-products")
- **AAFCO statement** for complete nutrition
- **Appropriate life stage** (puppy, adult, senior)
- **Ingredient quality** over marketing claims

## FAQ

### Is grain-free better?

Not necessarily. Choose grain-free only if your dog has a diagnosed grain allergy.

### How much should I feed?

Follow package guidelines based on weight, adjust for activity level.
